Stir-Fried Sweet Potatoes Recipe

This is the Recipe for the Stir Fried Sweet Potatoes which are a good source of healthy carbs and are tasty as well

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced sweet potatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they start to soften.
  3. Add the sliced onion, garlic, and ginger. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Serve hot as a side dish or over steamed rice for a complete meal.
